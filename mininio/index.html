How the math works — transparently

Mininio is a calculator, not a medical device. Here's exactly what it does and doesn't do.

✓ What it does

  • Takes the total grams of carbohydrate you logged and divides by your user-entered ratio (e.g., 100g ÷ ratio of 15 = 6.67 units)
  • Performs standard glucose-correction arithmetic using your own threshold, baseline, and divisor values
  • Saves your calculation history locally for you to review, copy, and track over time
  • Provides interactive charts to visualize your trends — frequency, top foods, carbs, glucose, and bolus results

✗ What it does NOT do

  • Never suggests, recommends, or adjusts your insulin ratios
  • Never calculates a dose based on internal logic or medical guidelines
  • Never claims to replace professional medical judgement
  • Never transmits your data to any server — there is no backend, no cloud, no analytics

Disclaimer: Mininio: Carb & Insulin Calc is a general wellness tool and mathematical calculator, NOT a medical device. Always consult your healthcare provider before adjusting your medication or diet.
